Avans University of Applied Sciences has several locations throughout the Netherlands and this is the Breda institution.
The oldest branch of the avans university of applied sciences group is the art academy AKV St. Joost in den bosch, which has existed since 1812.
The building on the Hogeschoollaan was opened on December 4, 2008 by Princess Maxima.
This is not the first or last time that political persons come to the university of applied sciences in Breda, both Rutte and King Willem Alexander have also been guests at Avans.
In 2011, 2012, 2014 to 2019, Avans was named the best large university of applied sciences in the Netherlands by the Keuzegids HBO.
