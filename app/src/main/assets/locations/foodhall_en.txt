In this culinary centre, you can find food from all over the world, from Japanese sushi and Brazilian streetfood to Italian pastas and Dutch bitterballen.
Everybody can find something delicious to eat here, whether you are a vegetarian or a meat-lover.
Do you want to take somebody out for a drink give them a drink through the foodhalls own gift card.
The foodhall is open from Tuesday till Sunday from 12:00 till 20:00.
Some restaurants might open later or close sooner but there are always a few open to serve between these times.
Are you already getting hungry? Then also check their website: https://foodhallbreda.nl/
