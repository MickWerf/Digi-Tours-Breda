Bouvigne Castle has a great mystery, none of the experts know how old the building is.
We know with certainty that it was occupied by the princes of Orange in 1614.
The castle has hosted many celebrities, such as Prince Philip William of Orange.
The castle is located in a man-made lake and is reached via a stone bridge.
The kiasteel has 3 gardens, the English garden, the French garden and the German garden.
There is also a burial chapel on the site where 2 disabled girls lie.
