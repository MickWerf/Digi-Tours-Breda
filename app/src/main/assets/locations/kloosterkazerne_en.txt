Turn left at the corner of Molenstraat and Oude Vest.

On the other side of this corner you will see a distinguished monument from the
history of Breda: the monastery barracks. It's part of the old
sister monastery St. Catherine Valley, which had been located here since 1295. It
current building dates from 1504. In 1645 the sisters became Norbertines
expelled from Breda to Oosterhout.
