This 10 meter tall statue was built and placed in may 2018 by artist Pascale Marhine Tayou.
The sculpture can be found in the center of a fountain in front of the justice building or Gerechtsgebouw on the Spoorlaan.
It was built using a metal frame and covering that with plastic plating and parts.
At the bottom of the fountain blue lines can be spotted shaping the different continents of the world.
This statue has become a symbol for diversity and the different cultures over the world, this is represented primarily by the masks that hang in the tree.
These masks were designed in the many different and unique styles from across the world.
