The city museum of Breda used to be a hostel for pilgrims and the homeless
these days the building is being used as a museum for many exhibitions.
The museum is accessible to wheelchair users and is free entry of children up to 18 years old.
The regular entry price is 12 euros but with a student card, you can enter for 5 euros.
For more information check out their website: https://www.stedelijkmuseumbreda.nl/en
