't Sas is a residential and shopping area in the center of the Dutch city of Breda that was opened in 2003.
't Sas is a shopping court consisting of shops, catering establishments and a hotel. The main entrance of 't Sas is on Lange Brugstraat, opposite the entrance of De Barones shopping center. The shopping area can also be reached via the entrances on the Havermarkt and the Torenstraat. 't Sas is located in the middle of Breda at the location where the Torenpassage used to be located.
The name 't Sas comes from Broodfabriek t' Sas. Name bearer Frans t 'Sas was a successful baker at the end of the 19th century. In 1877 he opened his bread factory in the current location of the shopping area.
